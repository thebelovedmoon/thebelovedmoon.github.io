Emii Shion started out previously as Suzu Puyotori, the penguin-turned-human who strives to connect with humans. she was once called to lead the kingdom held by her acquaintance, but had stepped down after 18 days due to an unforeseen illness.

fulfilling her promise to change after her recovery, she tried again; but as soon as she was about to fully lead another kingdom in its prime, she fell seriously ill, causing her to pass away.

however; with each passing, she was given a new life -- calling every moment a reincarnation of her past life to learn for the present. but each time she fails to protect something, the cycle renews -- giving her the title of "Reincarnated Phoenix Queen".

in her newest reincarnation, she was given a mission that has an undefined end goal -- alongside it, is to tame the "Cursed Legend" who was about to bring chaos and despair from Camelot and Avalon to the rest of Erden and beyond.
