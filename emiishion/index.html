the lore

in her past life, Emii Shion【絵美紫苑】👑🐣 failed to save her kingdom and died a tragic death attempting to save her subjects from a deadly plague. the gods saw that she was pure of heart and decided to give her another chance at life. they gifted her the ability to resurrect, but there was a catch. each time she dies, she is isekai-ed into a new world and returns as a new type of bird. in every single world, she is the Queen of a falling kingdom that she must save. each time she fails to save her kingdom and perishes, the cycle renews.
