it all started with Reiji Otoki and Eva Sanctuary.


墨宵🌸桜 Ch. started curating mangas because of a story from Manga Rabbit HoméNoba that revolves around the story of two gamers: an aspiring and a professional -- Reiji Otoki and Eva Sanctuary.

their support for each other has captivated its audience that even Sakura herself has been shifting gears towards delving more into these stories from other channels, such as AoHaru Manga Library and Kawaii RomCom Mangas.

the story is ranked 1st in the inaugural Mangas of 2022, and is currently racking 1.7mln views as of 15 Dec 2023, making it the most popular (and the very first video) of the channel.
