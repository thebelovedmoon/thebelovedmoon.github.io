the decision behind the new design


Sakura Sumiyoi 【墨宵🌸桜】's third (new) brand logo, debuted 1 Feb 2023

from these past few months, I've been eager to take my hand at designing a logo that fits well with its premise and symbolism. when I debuted the brand Sakura Sumiyoi 【墨宵🌸桜】, I aimed for it to become unique and unlike my mainstream counterpart, which focuses more on things beyond extreme (in short, all r18/nsfw instances). it also encapsulates my feeling of being "on the other side".

the initial logos for this brand was focused more on simplicity (something I usually do on some of my brands, like #MamaNyoSquad), but later they've become bland and don't fit very well. the typefaces also contribute to such blandness of the logos. (remnants of the previous logos are still present on this site and on the YouTube channel.)

Sakura Sumiyoi 【墨宵🌸桜】's second brand logo, debuted 22 Feb 2022

the decision to come up with this new design came from the inspiration of Mahoroba Youjo Kitan aka SAKURA（まほろば妖女奇譚） and my attempted sequel of it -- Life after Mahotan ～ まほたん後の人生 ～. the design idea I made for my attempted sequel which is supposed to match up the aesthetic of the original logos became the foundation of it.
