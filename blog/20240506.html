this admittedly is my very first 'proper' cosplaying experience that is closer to home, since I usually do cosplays in my free time!! caught wind of it during its first day so I thought I missed out on it; but upon realizing that there will be a second part the other day, I decided to pull it through and wing it -- here are some highlights to keep you in the loop!!

Bataan Anime Convention 2024

so admittedly, I'm currently living here in this province since my man Emi was also around here, but in terms of cosplaying shenanigans I've always been out of the loop. (for reference, the first proper cosplay I went to was Cosplay Matsuri 2023 - Day 3.) oftentimes, I usually cosplay myself in random places, just like what I did some times ago. but hey, it took me to places where people usually can't see cosplayers, which took the gamble out of me but it was definitely worth the patience!! I had a number of friends whom I can appreciate having around since it was them among those who encouraged me to cosplay in the first place.

I heard all about this convention from an artist fwen of mine who actually had a booth in there when I looked at her socials. (in actuality, I saw a number of cosplayers taking public transport before I realized, assuming I knew there was a local con there lmao) I thought that I was abt to reconsider giving another go whenever the time comes, but I quickly looked up the said convention and just realized they had a two-day event there, so I had to just check it out!! opportunity did rise tho as I was very eager to get in there with ~style~, so I prepped my cosplay stuff as I normally would.

only this time, except of transforming to my cosplay self inside the venue, I had let the neighborhood see my transformation from within it for the SECOND time. (yes, second time, since I did it before which confuses everybody around the perimeter lolz)

the main event

somehow I headed into the venue where the convention was taking place -- Vista Mall Bataan -- for lunch first before participating for real. it was good tho, had a few chicken and pizza with some drinks. had to head out for a bit cuz I had to adjust my breastpads (I got sweaty on the way there) but I somehow managed to snag some greetings to some of the fellows throughout the way -- with among em being curious on who I was for the first time. I could still remember the conversation I had with some of em who told me the exact same thing when they saw me walking outside the resto -- but atleast I had quite some talk throughout the process.

somehow I had some improvements on the way -- given the fact that I don't have the eyelashes I first wore back at Matsuri. main reason for this is that I was looking at the specs (aka the png) again and I realized that the eyelashes I used were waaaay too much for me so I had to take em off. I opted for a 'cleaner' approach this time -- using simple eyelashes and a mascara. I also had my contact lenses again after quite some looong time, since I wanted to try wearing em myself for a change of pace. everything still remained the same as it was tho.

regardless, I went ahead and visited around booths, to which I can recognize quite a few. (some of em were upstairs so I had to take an escalator.) from there, I finally found Haryu's booth -- the one that I discovered on X the moment I realized about the con taking place.
